Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Brahm AI ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Bimora AI. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Brahm AI mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, gender (optional)
  • Birth Details: Date of birth, time of birth, place of birth — used to generate your Kundali and astrological reports
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, chat messages (stored to improve AI responses)
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, app version

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized Kundali, horoscope, and astrological readings
  • To power the Brahm AI chat assistant with context about your birth chart
  • To store your chat history and saved Kundalis for future reference
  • To send notifications (Rahu Kaal alerts, etc.) if you opt in
  • To improve our AI models and app features

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers hosted on Google Cloud Platform (GCP) in the United States. Birth details and Kundali data are stored in our Supabase (PostgreSQL) database with encryption at rest.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI chat responses (your messages are processed but not stored by Google for training without consent)
  • MSG91 / 2Factor: Used for OTP verification via SMS
  • Sentry: Used for error monitoring (anonymized crash reports)

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data (30-day grace period)
  • Update or correct your birth details at any time via Profile settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Brahm AI is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@bimoraai.com

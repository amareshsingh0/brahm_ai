Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: You may cancel your subscription at any time. Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if you have not used the premium features.

1. Subscription Plans

Brahm AI offers monthly and annual subscription plans. Your subscription automatically renews at the end of each billing period unless cancelled.

2. Cancellation

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Profile → Billing section
  • After cancellation, you will retain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period
  • No further charges will be made after cancellation

3. Refunds

  • 7-day refund window: If you have not made significant use of premium features, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase
  • No refund after 7 days: Refunds will not be issued for subscription periods already used beyond the 7-day window
  • Annual plans: Partial refunds may be considered on a case-by-case basis for annual plans — contact support within 30 days of purchase

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at support@bimoraai.com with:

  • Your registered phone number
  • Date of purchase
  • Reason for the refund request

We will process your request within 5–7 business days.

5. Free Plan

The free plan is available indefinitely with no charges. No cancellation is required for the free plan.

6. Account Deletion

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active subscription or trigger a refund. Please cancel your subscription first before deleting your account.

7. Contact

For billing or cancellation queries: support@bimoraai.com
